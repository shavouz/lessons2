Why Choose Us?

At-Home Convenience, Music School Experience

We bring the full music school experience right to your home. Enjoy weekly private lessons, build your skills through musicianship classes, and share your progress with friends and family in our bi-annual student recitals. No commuting, no stress — just quality education, tailored to you.

Fair Pay, Supporting Live Music

We are a musician-led cooperative — not a profit led music school. That means our teachers are fairly compensated for their work, not underpaid while someone else takes an unfair cut. Taking lessons with us means that you're directly supporting the people who keep live music alive.

Real-World Musical Expertise

Our teachers aren't just educators — they're active, professional musicians performing and touring around the world. Our teachers are all performers and composers who do music full time, and won't read to you out of a dry textbook. Our lessons are shaped by of the stage, the studio, and the road, bringing you the knowledge you need to get to the next level.

Our Story

Music is OurUniversal Language

Our lesson programs are led by some of New York City's top musicians—active performers who tour internationally and bring their real-world experience directly into the classroom. This means students learn not just from textbooks, but from artists who live and breathe music at the highest levels.

Whether you're just starting out or getting ready for conservatory auditions, each lesson is personalized to fit your goals, learning style, and musical dreams. We believe that great teaching starts with understanding the individual, and we're here to guide you at every stage of your musical journey.

Our teachers have worked with students of all ages and levels, from complete beginners to seasoned professionals. With thousands of lessons taught around the world, we know how to meet you where you are and help you grow—and we take your progress seriously.
