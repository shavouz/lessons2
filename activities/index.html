Beyond the Lesson
Activities

We offer a range of activities designed to enrich the learning experience and build a vibrant musical community.

Student Recitals

Twice a year, students have the opportunity to showcase their progress and share their music with friends and family in a supportive and professional setting.

Musicianship Classes

Deepen your understanding of music theory, ear training, and composition in our group musicianship classes, designed to complement private instruction.

Performance Workshops

Prepare for auditions, exams, or public performances in our specialized workshops focused on stage presence, managing performance anxiety, and interpretative skills.

More information about schedules and enrollment will be provided to current students.
